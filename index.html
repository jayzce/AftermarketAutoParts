ABOUT US

Our story began with a passion passed down from father to son. Growing up, weekends often meant going to car shows with my dad where I discovered the beauty, power, and culture of cars.

That passion took a new direction when my dad bought a 1999 Ford F-150. At the time, finding OEM and aftermarket parts in the Philippines was a real challenge. Driven by the need to keep our truck running and looking its best, I began importing parts myself. Soon, friends and fellow car enthusiasts started asking me to source parts for them too.

What began as a simple hustle quickly grew through word of mouth. Today, that same passion and dedication has transformed into a trusted business committed to providing high-quality OEM and aftermarket parts that help car owners restore, customize, and elevate their rides.

At the heart of it all is the same love for cars that started years ago, a love that drives us to bring only the best for fellow enthusiasts like you.
